About Me

I'm Ignacio Esteban, a pediatrician and public health professional specializing in infectious disease epidemiology. With over 14 years of experience, I currently serve as a Senior Policy Manager in Epidemic and Pandemic Preparedness at Gavi, the Vaccine Alliance, and as an Associate Professor of Public Health at Austral University. My work focuses on vaccine-preventable diseases, vaccine policy and implementation, clinical trials, and comparative evaluation of vaccines.

About the Course

This 8-week course explores practical applications of Generative AI for productivity, research, and communication. Each module focuses on a unique aspect of AI tools and their integration into professional workflows.
