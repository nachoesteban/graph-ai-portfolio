About Me

Pediatrician specializing in Infectious Disease Epidemiology and Public Health professional with 15 years of experience. Master of Public Health at the Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health with a focus on Infectious Diseases and Vaccine Science and Policy. Primary research and work interests include the epidemiology of vaccine-preventable diseases, vaccine policy, and the comparative evaluation of vaccines, and vaccine clinical trials. Currently a Senior Policy Manager in Epidemic and Pandemic Preparedness at Gavi, the Vaccine Alliance and Associate Professor of Public Health at the Austral University School of Medicine.

About the Course

This 8-week live cohort course introduces participants to no-code AI tools for automating tasks in research and professional settings. It covers topics such as document generation, data analysis, literature review, chatbot creation, and scientific communication. Learners gain hands-on experience with AI assistants and build real-world projects to enhance productivity. The course is designed for researchers and professionals seeking to integrate generative AI into their workflows.
