About Me

Pediatrician specializing in Infectious Disease Epidemiology and Public Health with 15 years of experience. Master of Public Health from Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health focusing on Infectious Diseases and Vaccine Science & Policy. My work interests include vaccine-preventable disease epidemiology, vaccine policy, comparative vaccine evaluation, and vaccine clinical trials. Currently, I am Senior Policy Manager in Epidemic & Pandemic Preparedness at Gavi, the Vaccine Alliance, and Associate Professor of Public Health at Austral University School of Medicine.

About the Course

This 8-week live cohort course introduces participants to no-code AI tools for automating research and professional tasks. Topics include document generation, data analysis, literature review, chatbot creation, and scientific communication. Learners gain hands-on experience with AI assistants and build real-world projects to enhance productivity. Designed for researchers and professionals aiming to integrate generative AI into their workflows.
